## 🌟 Why Red Shield?

### 1. Post-Quantum Safe (ML-DSA)
Traditional ECDSA encryption will eventually be vulnerable to quantum computers. Red Shield utilizes NIST-standardized **ML-DSA** (Module-Lattice-Based Digital Signature Algorithm) to ensure that your identity and funds are protected from both classical and quantum threats.

### 2. SIMBA Protocol (Never Halt, Zero Loss)
Our Synchronized Intelligent Multi-node Blockchain Architecture ensures the network **never halts**. If nodes are partitioned, they gracefully handle local state, and intelligently merge upon reconnection using our proprietary PoN (Proof of Network) validation. 

### 3. Progressive Trust Scores (PTS)
Nodes aren't just trusted; they *earn* trust. By consistently proposing valid blocks and attesting faithfully, nodes build a Progressive Trust Score, graduating from Candidates to Guardians dynamically.
